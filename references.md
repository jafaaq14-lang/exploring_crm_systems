# Source Mapping and Reference Validation

To improve source quality and citation coverage, additional validation was performed to identify references that most directly supported the CRM research content. The objective was to locate sources that aligned closely with the factual claims made throughout the project and could serve as authoritative references for multiple sections.

---

## Section 1: What is CRM?

### Research Statement

> CRM represents both a strategy and software infrastructure designed to manage customer interactions, improve retention, and drive sales growth.

### Primary Supporting Sources

**TechTarget – What is CRM (Customer Relationship Management?)**

* Defines CRM as a combination of business practices, strategies, and technologies.
* Discusses customer retention, customer service improvement, and sales growth.

**IBM – What is CRM?**

* Describes CRM as integrated technologies used to manage customer relationships.
* Covers customer lifecycle management, lead generation, and sales pipeline management.

**Zendesk – What is CRM?**

* Explains CRM as a centralized platform for managing customer interactions.
* Covers forecasting, workflow automation, and pipeline management.
